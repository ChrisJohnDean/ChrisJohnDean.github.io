SNO has a colourful history of designing authentic Whistler fashions and gear for actionsport enthusiasts.

My father and I have teamed up to build off of Shirprint and Snomotions legacy. Contact us
For 20 years, Shirtprint studios was the only place in Whistler and Canada where original art was hand-silkscreened onto sustainably progressive activewear. Together, we have created SNO Whistler. SNO is a modern take on the brand, incorporating new age fashion trends into quality and sustainable performancwear.



Over time, this evolved to designing local-champion influenced Snomotion Whistler branded technical wear.
 With the growing urgency of climate change, our goal is to put 1/3 of profits towards pursuing solutions to save snow.
