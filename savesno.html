Our ultimate mission here at SNO is to keep the snow where it should be...
On the mountains!
 
 
By developing epic gear and fashions, SNO hopes to enable active lifestyles and a growing respect for nature. With more activity and more respect from more people, more commitment and solutions to save snow will follow... Creating a "Snoball" effect.
 
 
To start, we have introduced our first line of Whistler-class casual wear. Using progressive manufacturing partners and sustainable materials, these Canadian made products look and feel great while telling others you care about saving snow. More innovative performancewear will follow, designed with purpose, comfort and sustainability in mind.
